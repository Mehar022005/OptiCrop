About The Project

Opti Crop Agricultural Optimisation

The main purpose of Opti Crop is to develop an advanced software system that turns raw field data into clear, actionable recommendations. Instead of guesswork, farmers get a recommendation grounded in the same seven readings agronomists already track in the field.

N · P · KSoil nutrients
Temp · HumidityClimate readings
pHSoil acidity
RainfallWater supply

How it works

Opti Crop takes seven inputs — nitrogen, phosphorous, potassium, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall — and passes them through a trained machine learning model. The model compares your readings against patterns learned from historical crop-yield data to recommend the crop best suited to those exact conditions.

This helps farmers maximize yield, use fertilizer and water more efficiently, and make planting decisions backed by data rather than intuition alone.
